CONCEPT OF THE GAME

Usually, Air combat style video games are based around either helicopter, planes or sci-fi aircraft that attack or defend against an opposing side, they are fast-paced and require spatial awareness as well as quick decision-making skills to manoeuvre through terrains and opponents. The Project Pegasus is an air combat game which will resemble those characteristics, it include a multiplayer mode as well as a single player mode. They are quite different from one another but rely on the same graphic aspect and flying system. In single player : The player will here be asked to do many different missions, for example destroy all enemy planes. In this mode the player will be both the pilot and the gunner, as for the other planes they will be controlled by an AI. During the Campain, the player will complete missions and while he progresses throughout the game he will unlock new planes, skins and maps. In Multiplayer : When several players want to have fun together, they can have different roles, here the cooperation will be crucial. The pilot must be aware of the his fellow gunner so that he can shoot well. Our project will require that the players communicate with each other while rapidly making smart decisions to successfully pass the missions and unlock new elements of the game. Our game intends to be graphically as real as possible. We will put a lot of effort into the design and modelization of our characters, maps and planes so that they look real and of high quality. To obtain this result well spend a lot of time working on our 3D models as well as on our texturing. We decided that our game will not feature 3D models found on the internet, everything will be homemade. The flying simulation already relies on an advanced physical interface.
